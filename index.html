Dr. Biniyam Ayele Belete (Ph.D.)

**Dr. Biniyam Ayele Belete** is an engineer, academic leader, and cybersecurity researcher with over 15 years of excellence bridging advanced engineering and institutional service. Born in Harar in 1983, he earned his B.Tech and M.Tech in Electronics Engineering from the Defence University College of Engineering, and a Ph.D. from Adama Science and Technology University (ASTU), specializing in chaotic systems and secure data transmission frameworks.

His career reflects a powerful synergy between military field execution and cutting-edge theoretical research. Progressing from an Electronics Technician and Workshop Supervisor within the Ethiopian Air Force to academic leadership, he now serves as the permanent Head of Research and Development (R&D) and Instructor at the Ethiopian Air Force Academy, with dual instructional impact at the Defence Engineering College. His pioneering research integrates hyperchaotic systems, DNA cryptography, and deep learning for robust image encryption.
